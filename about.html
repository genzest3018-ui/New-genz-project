About GENZEST

We are building the next generation of financial toolkits. Fast, elegant, and completely private.

GENZEST was born out of a simple frustration: financial tools on the internet are slow, packed with heavy tracking code, outdated layouts, and painful pop-up ads. We stripped away the noise to bring you clean dashboard style calculations.

100% Privacy Locked

We do not collect numbers, track portfolios, or ask for account logins. All complex compound calculations run inside your browser client memory workspace instantly.
